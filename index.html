Team Members

Settings

When set, the app auto-sends a Slack notification when team members are assigned to new projects or phases.
Setup Instructions:
1. Go to api.slack.com/apps → Create New App → From Scratch
2. Name it "Starsound Scheduler" and select your workspace
3. Go to Incoming Webhooks → Activate → Add New Webhook
4. Select a channel (e.g. #integrations) and copy the webhook URL
5. Paste it above and hit Save Settings
